Privacy Policy for Galacosh Games

Effective Date: February 7, 2026

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for No Safe Tile, provided by Galacosh Games. This policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Information We Collect

Personal Data

We do not collect any personal data such as your name or email address.

Usage Data & Technical Data

We collect the following information:

  • Device Information: Model, Operating System, and identifiers.
  • Usage Analytics: Information about how you interact with the app, including game behavior and performance metrics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Features: Enhance your gaming experience and ensure smooth gameplay.
  • For Analytics: Understand user behavior to improve our app.
  • For Advertising: Deliver non-personalized ads in accordance with our advertising partners.

Third-Party Access

We may share your information with third-party services to help us operate our app. These services include:

  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics
  • Payment Processing: Google Play Billing
  • Authentication: Google Sign-In
  • Advertising: AdMob, Unity Ads
  • Cloud/Backend: Firebase
  • Social Integration: Instagram SDK, TikTok SDK, Facebook SDK
  • Crash Reporting: Crashlytics

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Children's Privacy

No Safe Tile is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Delete: You can request that we delete your personal data.
  • Opt-out: You can opt-out of data collection for advertising purposes.

AI-Powered Features

Our app does not currently utilize AI services that process user data.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

No Safe Tile operates on a freemium model with optional in-app purchases. We offer a refund policy of 14 days for one-time purchases. Payment information is processed through Google Play Billing.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: galacoshgames@galacoshgames.com

Thank you for using No Safe Tile!
