Our Story

We believe games should be bright, bold, and overflowing with joy.

The Mission

Born from the shared dreams of four friends in Bayburt, Galacosh Games is an independent studio dedicated to crafting vibrant and highly strategic experiences.

We want to build games that are mentally engaging yet instantly fun—colorful worlds where clever mechanics and dynamic puzzles take center stage. As a passionate team wearing many hats, we are unified by our love for deep gameplay, striking designs, and the true indie spirit.

🌍

Vibrant Worlds

We design colorful, eye-catching environments that bring our games to life. Every tile, creature, and puzzle is crafted to make our creative worlds truly pop off the screen..

🧩

Mind-Bending Puzzles

We build engaging grid puzzles that are instantly fun but fiercely challenging. Every mechanic is fine-tuned to deliver deep strategy and satisfying 'Aha!' moments.

🤝

Community First

Your feedback shapes our universe. We build our games alongside our community, relying on our awesome playtesters to help us refine every grid, rule, and level.
