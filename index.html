No Safe Tile

No Safe Tile blends the strategic depth of chess with the asymmetric rules of nature on a single grid. In this mind-bending puzzle game, your goal is to build a perfectly peaceful ecosystem. Your deceptively simple task is to strategically place animals and terrain elements so that no creature falls within a predator's range, herbivores stay fed, and all environmental restrictions are completely respected.
